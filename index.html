What is the Internet?

The Internet is the system of interconnected computer networks that link devices worldwide. It is a network of networks that links the private, public, academic, business, and government sectors around the world. The Internet carries a range of information over its various protocols, such as inter-linked hypertext documents (html pages like this one) and applications like, E-mail, phone or video sharing, and networks for file sharing.

Stats

The internet has been growing at an exponential rate ever since it was formed. It is predicted to continue this growth for the foreseeable future. The number of internet users has increased tenfold from 1999 to 2013. The first billion was reached in 2005. The second billion in 2010. The third billion in 2014.

World Wide Web

Many internet users confuse the internet with the "World Wide Web" when they are not the same thing. The internet is the process of linking computers together, where the World Wide Web is a protocol that is used to browse for information and display of html pages like This one!
